The Journey

From idea to working model

💡

The Problem

Specialist Sales needed a way to forecast FY27 ACV by product and by month — something that could update each month as new pipeline and closed data came in, without rebuilding from scratch.

🧱

Building the Foundation

We designed a two-component model: staged pipe × yield rates, plus intra-quarter ACV from deals with short sales cycles. Three-tier blending across FQ2/3/4 based on how much the pipe can be trusted.

📸

Adding the Snap Win Rates

We incorporated two years of weekly deal snapshots to train win rates by product × stage × days left in quarter — a much more precise signal than flat historical yield rates.

✅

Validation & Delivery

Backtested on FY27 Q1 (87.7% accuracy) and FY26 Q2 (87.8% on Service). Output: a fully formatted Excel dashboard that regenerates in 30 seconds each month.

The Collaboration

Each brought something different

The Sales Strategy Analyst
Specialist Sales
  • Domain expertise — how deals actually close, what matters by product

  • Real-world gut checks — "Service should be around $8M, not $4M"
  • Iterative feedback — what to improve, what to trust, what to question
  • The commit layer — rep calls that live outside the model
🤖
Claude (AI)
Anthropic · Claude Sonnet
  • Translated business logic into working Python code
  • Designed the modeling approach — blend weights, recency weighting, snap join
  • Debugged data quirks — competitor duplicates, 15 vs 18-digit IDs, column mismatches
  • Built the Excel dashboard with live formulas and formatting
  • Ran backtests and explained why accuracy was off — and fixed it

Key insight

Neither could have built this alone. The model needed real sales intuition to be calibrated correctly — and engineering depth to actually work at scale.

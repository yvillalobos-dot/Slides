Component 2 — Intra-Quarter

The invisible ACV

50–84% of ACV historically comes from deals that don't exist in the pipeline at quarter start.

3-Year Weighted Baseline

Historical intra-quarter ACV per product per quarter type — FY26 weighted 3×, FY25 2×, FY24 1×.

FY27 Trajectory Adjustment

If FY27 Q1 is tracking above or below FY26 Q1, the baseline scales accordingly — capped at 0.5×–2.0×.

Velocity Trend

If monthly ACV run-rate is accelerating vs prior year, the baseline gets an additional boost — capped at 0.7×–1.5×.

Monthly Split

Intra-quarter ACV is spread across Month 1/2/3 of each quarter using historical close patterns — so the monthly breakdown reflects how deals actually land.
